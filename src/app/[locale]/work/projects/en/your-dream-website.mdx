## Let's Create the Website of Your Dreams

A custom showcase website, a clean and professional portfolio, an original mobile app, a back office tailored to your needs, or a creative solution using modern technologies? Together, anything is possible. Let’s discuss your project.

## My Services:

- **Web/Mobile Development**: I can help you create a website, a mobile app, or a back office from scratch or work on an existing project, following best practices and standards (responsive design, multilingual websites, etc.).
- **Creative Development**: Need an original website, an innovative mobile app using modern 3D technologies, or another completely unique idea? Let's discuss and find the best solution for your creative project.
- **Consulting**: I can advise you on the right digital strategy, technologies to use, and best practices to follow.
- **Training**: Web and mobile development is a constantly evolving field. I can train you on the latest technologies and best practices.

## My Core Technologies

- **Next.js**: For front-end development with a component-based approach and dynamic routing.
- **React**: For creating interactive and responsive user interfaces.
- **Node.js**: For back-end development using microservices and REST APIs.
- **Feathers.js**: For building REST APIs and real-time services.
- **Three.js**: For creating 3D animations and interactive 3D scenes.
- **MongoDB**: For NoSQL database management with a document-based approach.
- **MySQL**: For relational database management using tables and relationships.
- **Once UI**: For designing flexible, highly customizable, modern, and responsive design systems.
- **Additionally**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, Python, C#, Unity, Unreal Engine, Blender...

## Our Collaboration

I’m available to work on projects of any size, from a simple web page to a complex mobile application. I’m flexible, responsive, and adapt to your needs and constraints. Feel free to contact me to discuss your project and how we can bring your ideas to life.
Whether by the day or for a full project, I adjust to your needs to help realize your vision.
